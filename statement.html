My Academic Statement

Currently my academic focus is on pursuing mechanical engineering, because of the wide variety of skills, and opportunities I will gain that no other engineering major offers. Throughout school I have 7 years of experience in which we competed in regional and national competitions. Over the past 2 years I have participated in TSA (technology student association) in which I have been able to create a vegetarian restaurant with my team and ultimately ended up placing 6th. Currently within TSA I am working on designing a marble maze to be mass produced as well as another website for our community. Through these organizations and projects I have been able to develop design, problem solving, and collaborative skills to allow me to complete hands-on engineering projects that have been preparing me with the engineering basics, ultimately leading me on a path to be able to design cleaner and more efficient cars, machines.
