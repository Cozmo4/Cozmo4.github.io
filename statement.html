My Academic Statement

A reflection of my learning journey, goals, and how I aim to grow through my academic and professional experiences.

Since a young age I wanted to become an engineer, and currently my academic focus is on mechanical engineering.Throughout life I have participated in robotics for 7 years, competing in regional and state competitions. Early last year I joined TSA and in 2025 I competed at TSA Nationals for Webmaster event where we were tasked with creating a vegetarian website, and ultimately ended up placing 6th. Currently I am still in TSA and working on designing a whole new website oriented around my local community as well as designing a puzzle box to be mass produced. These experiences have allowed me to develop design, prototyping, and core engineering skills but have also made me realize that I no longer want to focus on computer science and instead want to pursue mechanical engineering due to it being a broader field that I have a lot of experience in. Majoring in mechanical engineering will allow me to develop skills with the goal of further developing my STEM and engineering skills, and hoping to ultimately lead me into designing and creating cars.
