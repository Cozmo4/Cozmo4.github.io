Get to Know Me

I am a hard working and dedicated person who was born and raised in Louisville Kentucky. Currrently I do run track and cross country, as well as play soccer and a few sports on the side. During the year I enjoy traveling either for sports or for vacation due to the change of scenery. Additionally I am a person who tries to understand and imagine how things work which has left me spending the time when I'm not playing sports or traveling designing and creating projects, whether it is a website or 3D prints. Ultimately all of my travels and hobbies have shaped me into an outgoing, adventurous person.

Sports

Throughout my life I have played many different sports. Early on I started by playing baseball, soccer, and basketball, but ultimately fell in love with soccer which led me to play for almost 13 years for both my school and club. Last year I started running cross country and track to take on a new challenge.

Travel

Throughout my life, I have always enjoyed traveling, whether it’s an hour down the road to go on a hike, to another state, or even another country.
